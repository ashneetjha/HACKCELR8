About HACKCELR8

Revolutionizing the Future of Technology

HACKCELR8 is more than just a hackathon — it’s a 24-hour sprint of ideas, creativity, and collaboration. Over one intense day, teams of students and innovators will come together to brainstorm, build, and bring their boldest projects to life.

Date: September 15, 2025
Time: 9:00 AM IST
Venue: Mini Hall 1, SRM IST, Kattankulathur, Chennai-603203 Location
Team Size: 2-4 members
Registration Fee: ₹300 per team, ₹250 for IEEE members

Prize Pool ₹10,000

Team Size: 2-4

Tracks

Sustainable Development

Innovate for a greener future. Create solutions that address environmental challenges, promote renewable energy, and build sustainable communities.

AI/ML

Push the boundaries of intelligence. Develop cutting-edge algorithms, create intelligent applications, and explore the potential of machine learning.

Space Exploration

Reach for the stars. Design technologies for the final frontier. Work on projects related to satellite communication, rover navigation, and space data.

Open Innovation

Collaborate, share, and build on open-source technologies. This track is for those who believe in the power of community-driven development.
