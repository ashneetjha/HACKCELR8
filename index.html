About HACKCELR8

Revolutionizing the Future of Technology

24-hour sprint of ideas

HACKCELR8 is more than just a hackathon—it’s a 24-hour burst of ideas, creativity and collaboration. Over one intense day, teams of students and innovators brainstorm, build, and bring bold projects to life.
